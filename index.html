Who We Are

Peak Collective is a group of local mountain obsessives who live to share the Sierra Nevada with anyone willing to strap on a board or click into skis. Whether you're a first timer or a seasoned shredder, we've got a run for you.

What We Offer

Ski Lessons

Private and group lessons for all skill levels. From first timers to advanced carvers — our certified instructors have you covered.

Guided Tours

Explore hidden powder stashes and backcountry terrain with our local expert guides. No crowds, just mountain.

Gear Rentals

Top of the line skis, snowboards, boots, and helmets available daily. No need to lug your gear from home.

Group Packages

Planning a ski trip with friends or family? We offer custom group packages with everything included.
